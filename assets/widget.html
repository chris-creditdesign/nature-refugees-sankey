INTERACTIVE: Where refugees are going

Most of the 21.3 million refugees in the world as of end 2015 fled conflicts in Africa and Asia. The vast majority has sought refuge in neighbouring countries; only a small proportion headed to Europe or the United States. Just over half of refugees worldwide come from three countries, Syria, Afghanistan, and Somalia.

Sources: UNHCR and UN Department of Economic and Social Affairs. Data for flows compiled by N. Sander, Univ. Groningen, and R. Bauer, Vienna Inst. Demography.
